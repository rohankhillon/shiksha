Does Valley Prep have a community or religious affiliation?

No, Valley Prep is an independent, secular, coeducational, not-for-profit 501(c) 3 school, founded in 1957.

Valley Prep eighth grade graduates have recently attended the following high schools: Redlands High School, Redlands East Valley High School, Citrus Valley High School, The Webb Schools, Aquinas High School, Arrowhead Christian Academy, Loma Linda Academy, and Redlands Adventist Academy.
