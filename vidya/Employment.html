Our Five Institutional Commitments

VPS continues to implement the International Baccalaureate - Primary Years Program and is committed to providing each student with an exemplary education based upon the ideals of holistic education. Central to that work are our five “Institutional Commitments.” We are seeking faculty and staff who subscribe to these ideals and who wish to collaborate, innovate, and make a positive difference within the VPS community. Our Institutional Commitments are as follows:

Professional Learning Communities
Maintain a professional demeanor with students, parents, and colleagues.

Common, Guaranteed, Viable Curriculum
Develop and refine quality IB-PYP units to clarify intended teaching and learning.

Evidence of Learning and Performance
Implement assessment guidelines to establish clarity, consistency, and coherence. Use data or evidence to inform decision-making.

Great Teaching and Learning—Every Student, Every Day
Use appropriate instructional practices to support mastery of desired learning outcomes.

Integration of Technology (STEM-STEAM)
Utilize technology integration to extend and enrich student learning.
