VPS Board of Trustees

The VPS Board of Trustees is a team of nine elected individuals, the founder of Valley Prep, Mary Jane Auerbacher, and our head of school, John Black. Each dedicates his/her time time to Valley Prep for the benefit or our students. The board assists in implementing school programs, planning, and budgeting. This governing body ensures we use your tuition and donations responsibly.
