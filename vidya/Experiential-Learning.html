Experiential Learning

At Valley Preparatory School, we firmly believe in the importance of experiential learning as a means to further enhance our overall academic program. One way we provide these learning experiences is by taking part in international excursions.

3-Year Rotation

Our school provides a three-year rotation of trips, chosen to reinforce the philosophy of the International Baccalaureate Program, which prioritizes international mindedness and student-service opportunities. Please take a look at the details of the tours outlined below (subject to change from year to year). We utilize Explorica Educational Travel to arrange our excursions.

Year 1: Making a Difference from Coast to Coast—Costa Rica

This is an unforgettable 10-day adventure. You’ll discover breathtaking volcanoes, amazing wildlife, and serene lakes. You’ll learn what it means to change people’s lives forever. From San José to Tenorio National Park, your experience will leave a mark on the locals you’ll encounter as well as yourself.

Length: 10 Days
Cost: $2700 (subject to change)
When: Summer

Year 2: Québec & Montréal

Get a little taste of France with Québec’s European architecture, cobbled streets, outdoor cafés and crêperies. Stop at the Montmorency Falls—which are 98 feet taller than Niagara Falls—and head to Montréal to explore such memorable destinations such as Old Montreal, Notre-Dame Basilica, and the museum of archaeology.

Length: 5 Days
Cost: $1800 (subject to change)
When: Summer

Year 3: Galápagos Island Adventure

Jump from the northern to the southern hemisphere in an instant at the equator monument in Quito; fly to San Cristobal Island and swim among tropical fish; spot turtles; see penguins; and explore lava tunnels as you island-hop through the Galapagos.

Length: 11 Days
Cost: $4000 (subject to change)
When: Spring
