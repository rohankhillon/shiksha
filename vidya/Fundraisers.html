Fundraisers

Outstanding faculty. Small class sizes. Meaningful programs that enrich the whole child. High academic standards and expectations. These are the things that set Valley Preparatory School apart from all the rest. Your donations make it all possible.

Annual Fund

As is common in independent schools, tuition alone does not fully cover the costs associated with providing an exceptional education. The annual fund helps us bridge the gap between tuition revenues and what it actually costs to provide our students an unparalleled education that is student-centered, active, holistic, and encouraging.

As we continue to improve the experience at Valley Prep, the cost of fulfilling our mission grows each year. Your tax-deductible donation to the annual fund helps provide financial resources and flexibility that allow us the ability to offer the academic experience our families know and love at Valley Prep.

Whether you give $50 or $5,000, your gift has an immediate and meaningful impact. Every gift matters. Every donor makes a difference.

We recognize that your donation is made with great care and thoughtfulness, and for that we are thankful.
