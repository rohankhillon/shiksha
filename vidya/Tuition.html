Tuition

A Valley Prep education is an investment in your most priceless possession—your child. And thankfully, we have an amazing community of supporters who help defray some of the costs associated with fulfilling our lofty goals and purposes. 

Tuition Rates 2018-19

Tuition should be expected to increase annually by 3%, thereby allowing us to provide our teachers with modest salary and benefit increases. Next year’s tuition will increase by 2% (or approximately $250 for the year) so we can continue to provide our teachers with modest salary and benefit increases. As you make your decision regarding your child’s continued enrollment at VPS, we strongly encourage you to review the Curriculum Development Initiative.

Preschool, Prekindergarten, and Kindergarten: $11,362
1st–4th: $12,444
5th–8th: $13,526

Non-Refundable Deposit
We require a non-refundable deposit of $1000 per child to reserve a seat. The applicant pays this deposit directly to our business office upon completion of the enrollment contract (not Smart Tuition). The deposit will be deducted from the total tuition amount.

A sibling discount is available.

Tuition Payments
We collect tuition payments through Smart Tuition, LLC, which charges an annual administrative fee. All families must register with Smart Tuition, LLC® regardless of the payment plan selected.

Tuition Refund Insurance
Valley Prep partners with A.W.G Dewar Inc. to provide tuition refund insurance, which we require for the 10-month payment plan. The cost of the insurance is included in the stated 10-month tuition rate. In the event of a breach of the tuition contract, the insurance ensures that your financial obligation does not exceed 50% of the remaining balance. We also highly recommend tuition insurance (an additional fee of approximately 3.5%, arranged through the business office) for the one-payment plan, but we do not require it.

Educational Loans
Valley Prep partners with Your Tuition Solution Inc. for educational loans. Families that choose to finance tuition payments through Your Tuition Solution will select the one-time pay rate and then make monthly payments directly to this financial third party.

Daycare

Morning and evening extended day care is available (7 a.m.–6 p.m.) at Valley Prep. Daycare is included in the tuition cost for students in our preschool and prekindergarten programs. Due to licensing requirements, we require pre registration to determine staffing needs. For K–8 students, we offer the following two daycare options:

  • Pay the discounted amount of $1800 (sibling discount is available) for the entire year no later than Sept 1.
  • Receive monthly bills for occasional usage ($8 per hour—includes a late afternoon snack).
Other Significant Fees Not Included in Tuition

The following are other significant fees not included in the cost/payment of tuition:

  • Laptops—required for 2nd–8th graders
  • Daily lunch (approximately $6 daily)—paid monthly directly to Progressive Catering for Kids
  • Uniforms purchased through Lands-End (approximately $250 annually)
  • Overnight (mandatory) field trips in 4th–8th grade—costs vary yearly
  • Sacramento—4th grade ($1200*)
  • Pali Institute—5th grade ($600)
  • Joshua Tree, Catalina Island, or Colorado River—6–7th grade ($1200)
  • Washington D.C.—8th grade ($2500*)

*Paid directly to a 3rd party educational tour company

Financial Aid

We encourage new families requiring financial assistance to apply through Smart Aid. Returning families must reapply each year before April. At VPS, we use the information provided by Smart Aid to determine the amount of assistance we provide. The maximum per-family award is 25% of the annual tuition. It is the responsibility of all applicants to follow the procedure and provide the documents Smart Aid requires. We will only consider giving assistance to families who complete this procedure. We do not guarantee the same level of assistance annually.
