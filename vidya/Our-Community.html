Our Community

In Redlands, California, we enjoy small town charm just east of the big city of Los Angeles. Nestled just below the San Bernardino National Forest, we enjoy quaint shops, a variety of local restaurants, and easy access to hiking, biking, and equestrian trails.

Local Attractions

Downtown Market Night
Since its beginning in 1988, The Redlands Market Night is one of the most successful farmers markets in Southern California. Vendors vie to participate in this event that attracts thousands of visitors every week. Join us on Thursday evenings from 6 to 9 p.m. to enjoy over 150 food and merchandise booths, local shops, and great musical entertainment. 

Redlands Bowl
At 8:00 p.m. every Tuesday and Friday evening during the summer, the Redlands Bowl Summer Music Festival draws thousands to its world-class performances. Since its inception in 1924, the Bowl has been a special place in Redlands for families, friends, and music-lovers of all ages and backgrounds to gather and enjoy its superb programs.
